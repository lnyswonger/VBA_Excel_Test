
# XlWindowState Enumeration (Excel)

Specifies the state of the window.



|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **xlMaximized**|-4137|Maximized|
| **xlMinimized**|-4140|Minimized|
| **xlNormal**|-4143|Normal|
