
# XlMeasurementUnits Enumeration (Excel)

Specifies the measurement units.


## Version Information

Version Added: Excel 2007 



|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **xlCentimeters**|1|Centimeters|
| **xlInches**|0|Inches|
| **xlMillimeters**|2|Millimeters|
