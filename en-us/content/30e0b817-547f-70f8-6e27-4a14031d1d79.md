
# XlAxisGroup Enumeration (Excel)

Specifies the type of axis group.



|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **xlPrimary**|1|Primary axis group.|
| **xlSecondary**|2|Secondary axis group.|
